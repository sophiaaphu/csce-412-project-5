Emergency Fanum Tax Relief Program

This program provides temporary assistance to qualifying residents facing severe snack extraction and pantry destabilization from documented fanum tax events.

Tier A Relief

Up to 20% snack reimbursement for isolated, low-frequency tax incidents.

Tier B Relief

Up to 45% reimbursement and emergency chip allocation for repeated episodes.

Tier C Relief

Maximum support package for households experiencing critical nacho depletion.

Eligibility Guidelines

  • Applicant must reside in a federally recognized cooked zone.
  • Must provide evidence of unauthorized snack diversion.
  • At least one dependent with active school-lunch vulnerability.
  • No unresolved outstanding sigma fraud judgments.

Relief determinations are subject to audit by the Office of Snack Accountability and Vibe Stabilization.
